About

PerfectForm lets you train with an AI coach.

PerfectForm is a web application that puts an AI coach in your pocket. It uses a webcam to track your movements and give you the feedback you need to maximize your workout. It also lets you create and follow workout plans. PerfectForm was created by Jerry Xu, Jake Fleischer, Shalin Shah, and Daniel Dirksen for the Fall 2023 HackSC-X Hackathon.
